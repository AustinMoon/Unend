What We Do

We provide an online tutoring service primarily to non-native English speakers.

First,we check your English sentences.

Did you write some sentences in English? But, you don't know whether they are right or wrong? Please ask us!

Second,we answer questions about using English in your daily lives.

Are you wondering whether you can use this word in a certain situation? Please ask us!

 

Third,we check your English pronunciation.

Are you wondering whether your pronunciation is good? Are you preparing for an English speaking test, such as TOEFL or IELTS? If yes, please ask us! We have former TOEFL, IELTS speaking examiners!

Lastly, we proofread your papers.

Did you write a paper or a resume? Do you want to make your article perfect? If yes, please ask us!
